Steamed Buns Recipe

Ingredients Required Tools Step by Step Pictures

Ingredients

Dough:

  • 250 g milk
  • ½ cube fresh yeast (approx. 20 g), crumbled
  • 50 g soft butter
  • 1 egg
  • 30 g sugar
  • 475 g white bread flour (e.g., Zopf flour)
  • 1¼ tsp salt

Glaze (Milk Mixture):

  • 300 g milk
  • 30 g butter
  • 100 g heavy cream

Required Tools

  • Mixing bowl
  • Thermomix or stand mixer
  • Measuring cup
  • Ovenproof dish (22x28x4 cm)
  • Aluminum foil or ovenproof lid
  • Spatula or spoon
  • Kitchen scale
  • Brush (optional)
  • Clean kitchen towel
  • Oven

Steps

1. Prepare the Yeast Base

Add milk and crumbled yeast to the mixing bowl. Warm for 3 minutes at 37°C on speed 2.

2. Make the Dough

Add butter, egg, sugar, flour, and salt to the bowl. Knead for 3 minutes using kneading mode.

3. Let the Dough Rise

Transfer dough to a bowl, shape into a ball, cover, and let it rise for 1 hour or until doubled in size.

4. Portion and Proof

Divide the dough into 16 balls, place them in a greased dish, cover, and let them rise again for 30 minutes.

5. Prepare the Glaze

Warm milk, butter, and cream for 3 minutes at 40°C, speed 2.

6. Bake the Buns

Pour the glaze over the buns, cover the dish, and bake at 200°C for 20-25 minutes.
